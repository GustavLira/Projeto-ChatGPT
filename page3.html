What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a computer program that simulates human conversation through voice
commands or text chats or both. It can be integrated with various messaging plataforms
like Facebook Menssenger, WhatsApp, Wechat, etc. And can be used for a variety of
purposes, such as customer service, enternainment, and e-commerce
